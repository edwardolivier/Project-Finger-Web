Create Your Secret Key

This key encrypts your passwords on the device. It is never sent anywhere — it stays between you and your dongle.

Write this key down and keep it somewhere safe. If your dongle ever wipes itself after failed scans, you will need to enroll again and this key lets you prove it is your device.

After this many wrong fingerprints in a row, all passwords and fingerprints are permanently wiped.
